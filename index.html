Vexil

Vexil (named for Vexillology) is a Swift package for managing feature flags (also called feature toggles) in a flexible, multi-provider way.

Privacy

Your email address will be added to a Mailchimp mailing list and will be sent emails related to:

  • the initial TestFlight beta release for iOS
  • the initial TestFlight beta release for macOS
  • and details related to our launch.

You will receive less than five emails total.

After launch the mailing list will be deleted and our record of your email address will be removed. If you choose to join the TestFlight beta then you would need to supply your details separately there.

For more information on how Mailchimp will use your email address see their Privacy Policy.
